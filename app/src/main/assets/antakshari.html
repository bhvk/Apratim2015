Antakshari

We've all enjoyed the antakshari on our bus rides, family outings, picnics and honed our impromptu skills at singing those well-known songs but now is the time to showcase the acquired talent and also a chance to win a reward for the same. Even if you've lost touch with the much adorned game, you can always start again and relive those moments of spontaneity and fun.

Rules

  1. The contest will be conducted in rounds.
  2. A preliminary round (written) might be conducted.
  3. A team shall comprise of maximum 2 members.
  4. Any kind of vulgarity and blasphemy will lead to disqualification.
  5. Decision of judges will be final.
  6. Registrations can be done online as well as on the spot.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
