Brain-Quiilla

A quiz for electronics enthusiasts which will tickle their grey cells. The questions will be from basic electronics, keeping in mind that students from al years are able to participate.

Rules

  1. A team shall consist of maximum three students. Team members can be from different colleges and year.
  2. Participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic instruments.
  3. Questions shall be in form of multiple choice, true/ false, visuals and specific-answer question etc.
  4. Audience shall not give any hints or clues to the competitors.
  5. The quiz will be held in multiple rounds, including an Elimination Round.
  6. Judges' decision shall be treated as final and binding on all and sundry; and the judges reserve all rights to eliminate the participation of any team that indulge in any informal activities.
  7. Arguments are strongly discouraged.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
