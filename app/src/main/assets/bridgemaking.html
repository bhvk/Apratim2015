Bridge It

Bridges are the most useful and magnificent structures of the modern civilization.Apratim 2015 brings the annual bridge competition that would test your ability to design and construct the most efficient bridge within within the specifications using straws,pins and thread.Think innovatively and get the best out of your creative minds.So unleash your imagination.

Rules

TEAM SPECIFICATIONS:

  1. A maximum of 2 members are allowed in a team.
  2. Eligibility: All students with a valid student id card from their respective colleges are allowed to participate.

BRIDGE SPECIFICATIONS:

  1. Construct a bridge (road/pavement above the truss) of length 450-600 mm using straw and adhesives.
  2. The roadway should be continuous.
  3. The width of the bridge at any point shall not be more than 180-250 mm.
  4. Total height of the deck from footing should be between 130-160 mm.

Loading procedure and grading criteria:

  1. Loading will be done using a weigh at the center of the bridge.
  2. All construction and material requirements will be checked prior to testing. Bridges failing to meet these requirements will be disqualified.
  3. Deflection Test: The Bridge will be tested for a deflection.
  4. All the dimensions mentioned are acceptable within an error range of -5% to +5 %. Any failure in adhering to the specifications will lead to immediate disqualification.
  5. Aesthetic view of the bridge will also be considered while making the decision.
  6. All decisions of the judges shall be final.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
