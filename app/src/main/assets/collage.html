Collage Making

If you happen to know how to make bits and pieces of paper turn into something magnificent and ingenious this event is just for you. Make the finest of all colours and prints you can find and let your vision work the magic. Put together ordinary and make it extraordinary.

Rules

  1. Maximum number of members is 3.
  2. Time limit is 1 hour.
  3. Colors will be provided.
  4. Material(newspapers) will be provided.
  5. Registrations can be done online as well as on the spot.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
