Conventional Debate

A Debate "old and traditional" yet "unparalleled and most loved" literary event. The event maybe traditional but the twists and turns are short to be untraditional. If you have these "influential" skills this is your arena. Logic and thought, command on speech is what is going to take you through the eliminations leading to you finals. The place to prove your mettle!

Rules

  1. Topic will be given 15 minutes prior to the commencement of the debate.
  2. Time limit is 2+1 minutes for prelims and 4+1 for finals with rebuttal time of 1 minute after every speaker.
  3. Individual participation only.
  4. The participants will have the freedom to pick their motion.
  5. Criteria of judging will be content, logical reasoning, presentation and delivery.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
