Face Painting

If you think using paper to paint your strokes is too mainstream here is a chance for you to treat your friend's face as a canvas and let your imagination show on his/her face.If you've painted a canvas with no expression and turned it into a masterpiece imagine what you could with a canvas with expressions of its own.Give your friend a makeover with your artistic skills and bag prizes for the same.

Rules

  1. Maximum number of members is 2.
  2. Time limit is 1 hour.
  3. Colors will be provided.
  4. Registrations can be done online as well as on the spot.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
