Fashion Show

You know you are the showstopper of your life, so bring that dazzle to the stage. With the lights setting the stage on fire and music synchronizing with your stride, let the world stare and leave them in awe of your confidence and mesmerize the audience. So bring together your hunks and divas and show them what you got because this is where your style pays off.

Rules

Round 1:Prelims

  1. Time Limit : 5-7 minutes.
  2. Member limit: up to 25 members including designer and choreographer.
  3. No marks for the costumes in prelims.
  4. Vulgarity of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification.
  5. Teams should bring music track in pen drives as well as CDs.

Round 2:Finals

  1. Time Limit : 15-20 minutes.
  2. Member limit: up to 25 members including designer and choreographer.
  3. Prelims track should be a part of the final track.
  4. Green room will be available behind the stage.
  5. The decision of judges shall be final.
  6. Use of flammable substances like candles, matches or cigarettes or any alcoholic product during performance on stage is not allowed and will result in disqualification.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
