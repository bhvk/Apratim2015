Folk Dance

Being a part of a diverse country we have no shortage of heritage is full of the vibrancy of colours and enthusiasm of its holders . If you're one of those few cherish and celebrate our culture, we invite you to showcase your cultural pride and let people know what a magnificent country they live in.

Rules

  1. Vulgarity of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification.
  2. Stunts related fire and water are not allowed.
  3. Teams should bring their music track in Pen drives as well as CDs.
  4. The decision of the judges shall be final.

Round 1:Prelims

  1. Time Limit : 4-5 minutes.
  2. Member Limit : 5-20 member.
  3. Stage Limit : 5-20 member.
  4. No marks for costume and props in prelims.

Round 2:Finals

  1. Time Limit : 8-12 minutes.
  2. Member as well as stage limit : 5-25 members.
  3. Prelims track should be part of the finals track.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
