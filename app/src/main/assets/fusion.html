Fusion Band

The ambience whose air carries a fusion of the tunes of various instruments in sync where you are drifted to a world of amalgamated flavours of east and west.Where melody rules nonchalantly.

Rules

  1. Team limit is 7 members.
  2. Time limit is 12 minutes including sound check.
  3. Original compositions will be appreciated and fusion of different genres is expected.
  4. Judging criteria: innovation of different genres, amalgamation of different rhythms and beat pattern.
  5. Light fusion of different genres expected. Rock and metal not allowed.
  6. Any kind of vulgarity and blasphemy will lead to disqualification.
  7. The decision of judges will be final.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
