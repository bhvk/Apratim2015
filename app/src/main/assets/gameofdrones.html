Game of Drones

The participants are required to make a quadcopter that is stable, and can pass the various hurdles in its way and make it till the end.

TASK SPECIFICATIONS:

  1. Build your Quadcopter to travel from source to destination crossing various interruptions, hurdles, etc., along the path of journey.
  2. Skills to be tested: Innovation,Designing,Efficiency,Stabilization,Team Work

PARTS ALLOWED:

  1. Complete Quad copter:Quad Frame,Radio Devices,Stabilization Unit,BLDC Motors,ESCs,Propellers
  2. Additional Accessories: Gimbals can contain maximum of 2 Axis,Relaxation is given with Manual or Autonomous mode of operation depends on the ease of access of the teams.Landing Gears:Retractable landing gears should fix with the dimensions,Retractable Landing gears can be used in this case make sure that the Quad copter can fly in the hurdles without getting any accidents.

QUADCOPTER SPECIFICATION:

  1. The complete quad copter should fix inside the box of dimension 625x625x625 (lxbxh). All dimensions are in mm.
  2. The dimension includes the Gimbals arrangement and the Landing skids. The relaxation will be provided as per the specifications.
  3. There is no restriction in making use of the Quad frame material or the Ratings of the BLDC Motors, Electronic Speed Controllers, Propellers, Batteries and Weight of the Quad copter.
  4. READY MADE TOY QUADCOPTER KITS ARE STRICTLY RESTRICTED.

ARENA

  1. The whole championship arena is divided into various stages of evaluation.
  2. The flying of quad copter will be from the specific Source to Destination.
  3. There will be enough time provided for the teams to charge the batteries at the completion of every stage. The requirement of time of charging the batteries during the conduct of competition will not be entertained.
  4. The teams can skip the stages in which they think it is not possible for them to complete.

Rules

  1. The Quadcopter event is open to the students of all the Departments and there are no restrictions over the branch and year of studying.
  2. A team should comprise of maximum 2 members.
  3. Quadcopters are to be built according to the specified dimensions given in the Specifications tab.
  4. The hurdles will be built according to the dimensions of the Quadcopter.
  5. The performance of the Quads in all the Stages/Arena will be evaluated individually.
  6. The Quadcopter performing EXCELLENT in all the stages overall when put together is declared as WINNER.
  7. A team can take part with only one Quad.
  8. Replacement of the parts of Quad will be allowed in case only if accident occurs.
  9. Timers will be paused only at the Checkpoints, and NOT at the Stage/Arena at any cause (Timeouts are provided according to the Problems arising during the flight).
  10. The retrace of a particular Stage/Arena is entertained with some negative points if the problem cannot be troubleshooted, or in case of accidents.
  11. Individual participation in flying session of the team members will also be evaluated.
  12. If the Quad is found making damage to the arena, Judges reserve rights to eliminate a particular Stage/Check Point/Complete Participation.
  13. The organizers reserve the right to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in rules if any will be highlighted in the website.
  14. No test practice will be allowed in the competition arena but they are providing with separate arena.
  15. Any team that is not ready at the time specified even after relaxation according to the problem occurred will be eliminated from the competition.
  16. Participants shall not be allowed to be part of more than one team.
  17. The competition comprises of several rounds.
  18. Judges decision shall be treated as final and binding on all and sundry.
  19. Arguments are strongly discouraged.
  20. Judges reserve all rights to eliminate the participation of any teams who indulge in any informal activities.
  21. The Teams are responsible for all the materials they bring for the competition.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
