Graffiti

"Graffiti is one of the few tools you have if you have almost anything. And even if you don't come up with a picture to cure world poverty you can make someone smile while they're passing." Writing graffiti is the most honest way of being an artist. Give the usual art a backseat and let aerosol art work with your imagination and let your graffiti be a revolution.

Rules

  1. Maximum number of members is 4.
  2. Time limit is 2 hours.
  3. Colors will be provided.
  4. Registrations can be done online as well as on the spot.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
