Group Dance

As entertaining as ever, the synchronized motion of bodies to the beats of music has never failed to mesmerize the audiences. The best of the dancers put up their A-game to present to you a show you will remember for a long time. If you're one of those dancers who think they can do so, we've got the right opportunity to leave the audience flabbergasted and also win for the same. Group Dance competition will be in 2 rounds.

Rules

  1. Team size: minimum 5 members and maximum 25 member are allowed in a team.
  2. The decision of the judges shall be final.

Elimination Round:

  1. Time Limit : 4-5 minutes.
  2. Teams will have to perform to a suitably edited part of the music from their main performance.
  3. The music which is used in the elimination round has to be a part of the final round.
  4. No marks for costumes and props in prelims.
  5. Vulgarity of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification.
  6. Stunts related to fire and water are not allowed.
  7. Teams should bring their music track in pen drives as well as CDs.

Finals Round:

  1. Teams present their prepared performances.
  2. Time limit: 8-12 minutes ( music piece duration).
  3. Costumes and props will be included in the judging criteria this round.
  4. The decision of judges shall be final.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
