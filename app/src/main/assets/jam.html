JAM

Are you a grammar nazi? Then this is your fortress. JAM is a major fun event in any literary setup. This is the only competition in which you can talk gibberish all you want and it will be acceptable. They say to err is to human, but here if you err, you lose. So if you are worthy of it, the prize is yours.

Rules

  1. Participants have to speak continuously without stuttering, stammering or making grammatical errors.
  2. While a participant is speaking the other has to look for errors JAM when the error is made.
  3. Participant who clocks maximum speaking time wins.
  4. JAM master is God. Period.
