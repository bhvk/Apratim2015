Mime

Your expressions speak a thousand words and how apt this platform would be to showcase all you want without uttering a single word.It takes an artist to talk without actually speaking and if you are that artist Apratim15 recognizes it and gives you a chance to tell the world your story, but without words.Come Mime and leave them speechless.

Rules

  1. Empty to empty stage limit is 7-15 minutes.
  2. All participants should be dressed in black with their hands and face painted white (use of white gloves is allowed).
  3. Reliance on mode of speech is strictly prohibited.
  4. The use of props is strictly prohibited.
  5. The act should be in the limits of decency.
  6. Team limit is 10 members including the member in charge of music.
  7. Teams should bring their own tracks in pen-drive as well as CDs.
  8. The decision of the judges will be final.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
