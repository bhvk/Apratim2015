National Parliamentary Debate

A National Parliamentary debate is one of the most traditional and sacred debates. It's where men are made. Total politics and manipulation, it tests your thinking ability on the trot. Think you can manipulate the house and get the verdict in your favor? Come test yourself among the best of the best.

Format

  1. Teams of 3 accompanied by one adjudicator per team.
  2. Conventional format of leader, deputy leader and whip.

Rules

  1. Topic is given 30 minutes prior to round.
  2. Points of information, points of order, points of inquiry are allowed.

Rules like speaking time and provision of rebuttals are subject to change as per the adjudicators and as per debate.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
