Skit

A small storyline based on social issue, variety of characters and innate talent to perform. All this infused with a bit of humour, satire and creativity is what it takes to awe the viewers along with best skills of the actors.

Rules

  1. Time limit: 12-18 minutes (empty to empty stage).
  2. Team limit is 20 members.
  3. No restriction on number of musical instruments used.
  4. Use of fire, water and explosives is strictly prohibited.
  5. Vulgarity , blasphemy ,offensive content against any particular sect/public figure is strictly not permissible; points may be deducted for the same.
  6. Decision of judges will be final.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
