Slump it

Here comes the real challenge for all of you.Making houses with sand on the beaches by constant tapping with hands was a fun.Here is the real task to check the stability of your material.You have to create a slump of the concrete and the team which makes the true slump will be the winner.

CONDITIONS

  1. Material will be provided by organizing team. The students are required to do slump test.
  2. Ratio of the concrete will be provided on the spot and slump has to be made according to the grade.

Rules

  1. 2 Students per team.
  2. The slump of concrete mix shall be 75mm.
  3. The group having slump value closer to the criteria will be winner.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
