Solo Dance

If your feet are Eveready to dance to the beat and you can mesmerize the crowd with your moves then we have this event specifically for you dance lovers. Groove to the beat and charm your way to bag the big prize.

Rules

Round 1:PRELIMS

  1. Track limit: 2-3 minutes.
  2. No marks for props and costumes.
  3. Stunts related to fire and water are not allowed.
  4. All dance forms are allowed.
  5. Participants should bring their music track in pen drives as well as CDs.
  6. Vulgarity of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification.
  7. Registrations can be done online and on the spot.

Round 2:FINALS

  1. Track limit: 4-5 minutes.
  2. All dance forms are allowed.
  3. Stunts related to fire and water are not allowed.
  4. Participants should bring their music track in pen drive as well as CDs.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
