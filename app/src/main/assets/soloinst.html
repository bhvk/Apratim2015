Solo Instrumental

The event that awaits those beautiful chords to be plucked, tambourine to be thumped and the keys to be played. If you have the dexterity of weaving melody with your hands, here is the opportunity.

Rules

Round 1:PRELIMS

  1. Time limit: 2-3 minutes.
  2. No instruments will be provided.
  3. Stunts related to fire and water are not allowed.
  4. Any kind of vulgarity will lead to disqualification.
  5. Decision of judges will be final.
  6. Registrations can be done online and on the spot.

Round 2:FINALS

  1. Time limit: 4-5 minutes.
  2. No instruments will be provided.
  3. One accompanist allowed.
  4. Decision of judges will be final.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
