Solo Singing

If you can weave the rhythm and harmony of music with your melodious and rocking voice then come be the prima Donna or the Primo Cuomo. With your stage and unbounded talent hypnotize the audience with your voice and make the world stop to hear you sing.

Rules

Round 1:PRELIMS

  1. Time limit: 2-3 minutes.
  2. Hindi / English / Punjabi songs are allowed.
  3. No instrument/karaoke is allowed.
  4. Any kind of vulgarity and blasphemy is not allowed and will lead to disqualification.
  5. Decision of judges will be final.
  6. Registrations can be done online and on the spot.

Round 2:FINALS

  1. Time limit: 4-5 minutes.
  2. Hindi / English / Punjabi songs are allowed.
  3. One accompanist/Karaoke is allowed.
  4. Any kind of vulgarity will lead to disqualification.
  5. Decision of judges will be final.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
