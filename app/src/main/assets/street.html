Street Play

Do you remember the campaign you read about in some newspaper? No,but you do remember the nukkad natak for the same cause.Satire,comedy,drama catches the eye and leaves a greater impact than any number of words written.So here's your chance to express that one change that you need to bring and do so dramatically.Pick any cause you wish to address and present it to the public in your act and let them see what their society is dealing with.Bring in the "Passion and the Power to Change."

Rules

  1. Script should be original/self-written and not be copied, if found guilty, would result in disqualification of team.
  2. Team limit is 20 members including member in charge of music.
  3. Substitutions are not allowed.
  4. No restriction on use of musical instruments. Marks will be on creative use of musical instruments and not on their number.
  5. No props other than saropas and sticks are allowed. Other props can be used with the permission of coordinator. Marks will be on creative use of props and not on their number.
  6. Vulgarity, blasphemy, offensive content against any particular sect/ public figure is strictly not permissible; points may be deducted for the same.
  7. Language used can be Hindi or English.
  8. Any electronic device or recorded music is not allowed.
  9. Negative points for exceeding time limit.
  10. Decision of judges will be final.

There will be two rounds:

  1. Elimination Round:- Time limit: 10 min. Perform a part of the final performance. On the basis of the performance 4 teams will be qualify for the final round.
  2. Final Round:- Time limit: 18-22 min.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Cerificates of participation will be given to all the paricipating teams.
  2. Certificates of Excellence and prize money will be awarded to the winning teams.
